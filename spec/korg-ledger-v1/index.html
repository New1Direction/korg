Proof of what an AI agent actually did.

AI agents write code, move money, and touch production — and nobody can independently verify what one actually did. korg is the open standard that fixes it: a tamper-evident ledger of agent actions, and a Gold Seal certificate anyone can re-verify offline, with zero trust in the tool that produced it.

Why you can trust a green check

  • You don't trust us. Verification recomputes everything from the bytes you hold — no server, no network, nothing phones home.
  • Three independent implementations reproduce one frozen oracle, so a check in one is corroborated by two others written from the same spec.
  • The summary can't lie: a Gold Seal's human-readable summary is re-derived from the events and rejected on any mismatch.
